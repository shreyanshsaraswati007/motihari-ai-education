Motihari-AI-Education is an AI-driven community analysis project focused on Motihari (East Champaran), Bihar, designed to understand why educational and opportunity gaps exist and how they can be reduced using data-based insights. The project combines publicly available Indian datasets on population, literacy, gender ratio, schools, teacher availability, and internet access, and applies machine-learning models to uncover patterns that are not visible through traditional analysis. By using clustering algorithms, the project compares Motihari with other Bihar districts to identify whether it falls into a high-opportunity or low-opportunity group. A regression model is then used to determine which factors most strongly influence literacy levels. The results show that Motihari belongs to a lower-opportunity cluster characterized by lower literacy and weaker digital access, and that internet penetration has a strong positive relationship with literacy rates. This highlights that improving digital access, especially in rural and under-resourced communities, can directly support educational growth.
